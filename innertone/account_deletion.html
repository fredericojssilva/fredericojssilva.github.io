Request to Delete Your Innertone Account and Data

At Innertone, we respect your privacy and give you control over your data. If you would like to delete your account and associated personal data, you can do so using one of the following methods:

  1. In-App Deletion: Open the Innertone app, go to Settings → Account → Delete My Account. This option will immediately begin the process of permanently deleting your account and all associated data.
  2. By Email: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by emailing us at fredericojssilva@gmail.com using the same email address used to create your Innertone account. Please include the subject line: Account Deletion Request.

Once we verify your identity, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 7 days.

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at fredericojssilva@gmail.com.
